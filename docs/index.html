Conflict of Interest

Capitalism Kills

It's finally the day. You and your crew have been planning meticulously for months. You're going to rob the Urbanopolis National Bank, and you're all going to be rich. There's only one problem:

Every other gang in the city had the same idea.

For the same bank, on the same day, at the same time.

Select your loadout in the planning stage. During gameplay, find bags of money and take them back up to your getaway car. Take out your rival gang members and steal their moneybags before they make it back. Near the end of the countdown, get to your car and get in it - or you're stuck with the cops!
